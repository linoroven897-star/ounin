Our Beginning

Ounin was founded in 2018 by a team of engineers and professional chefs who shared a common frustration: why should restaurant-quality cooking remain confined to commercial kitchens?

After years of development, testing, and refinement, we created our first steam oven — a machine that combines precise temperature control, advanced steam technology, and elegant design.

Our Technology

Every Ounin oven features our proprietary Precision Steam System™, which maintains exact humidity levels throughout cooking. Whether you're baking artisan bread, roasting a perfect chicken, or steaming fresh seafood, our technology ensures consistent, restaurant-quality results.

Combined with smart home integration and intuitive controls, Ounin ovens are designed to make professional cooking accessible to everyone.
