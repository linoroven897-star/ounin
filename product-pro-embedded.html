Care & Maintenance

Simple steps to keep your Pro Embedded performing at its best for years.

🧽 After Each Use

  • | ?Wipe the interior with a damp, soft cloth after each use
  • | ?Remove and rinse the water tank; air-dry before reinserting
  • | ?Clean the door glass with a non-abrasive cleaner
  • | ?Run a quick steam cycle with plain water monthly

🧴 Deep Clean (Monthly)

  • | ?Run descaling cycle using Ounin descaling agent
  • | ?Remove and hand-wash all trays with warm soapy water
  • | ?Wipe the steam vent with a cotton swab
  • | ?Inspect door gasket for debris and clean if needed

💡 Tips for Longevity

  • | ?Use only distilled or filtered water to reduce scale buildup
  • | ?Never use harsh chemicals or steel wool on stainless surfaces
  • | ?Keep the exterior free of grease and moisture
  • | ?Update firmware via the app for latest features
