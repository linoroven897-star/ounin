Care & Maintenance

Your Elite Bundle is an investment — follow these steps to keep it performing at its best.

🧽 After Each Use

  • • Wipe the interior with a soft, damp cloth
  • • Remove and rinse the water tank; air-dry completely
  • • Hand-wash all accessories with warm soapy water
  • • Use the bamboo board for handling hot items from the oven

🧴 Monthly Maintenance

  • • Run a full descaling cycle using Ounin descaling agent
  • • Use the water filter pitcher for cleaner steam production
  • • Wipe the door glass and check the gasket seal
  • • Update firmware via the Ounin app for best performance

💡 Caring for Accessories

  • • Never put silicone handles in the oven without a tray
  • • Hand-wash the bamboo board; never soak or put in dishwasher
  • • Clean the water filter pitcher weekly with white vinegar
  • • Store all accessories in a cool, dry place

⚠️ Troubleshooting the Elite Bundle

  • • Weak steam? — Use filtered water; run a descaling cycle
  • • Accessory won't fit? — Confirm you're using the Pro (75L) rack positions
  • • Bamboo board warped? — Season with food-safe mineral oil
  • • WiFi won't pair? — Reset in Settings > Network; restart router if needed
